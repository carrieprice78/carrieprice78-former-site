Expertise and Services

Bibliographic Literature Database Expert Searching

I create and process expert searches in PubMed, the Cochrane Library, and clinical trials registries for policy, review, and guideline development.

Bibliographic Literature Database Instruction

I instruct synchronously or asynchronously on using PubMed, the U.S. National Library of Medicine and the National Center for Biotechnology Information's platform for biomedical literature.

Citation Management

I establish and maintain citation libraries for your research or topics of interest. Programs used: EndNote, Paperpile, Mendeley, Zotero.

Desktop Publishing

I perform copyediting, proofreading, accessible formatting, and resume review.

Peer Review

I peer review your existing search strategies according to the Peer Review of Electronic Search Strategies (PRESS) Guideline Statement. (Reference: McGowan, J., Sampson, M., Salzwedel, D. M., Cogo, E., Foerster, V., & Lefebvre, C. (2016). PRESS peer review of electronic search strategies: 2015 guideline statement. Journal of clinical epidemiology, 75, 40-46. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jclinepi.2016.01.021

Researcher Profiles

If you are a researcher in healthcare or STEM, you may want to establish researcher profiles for grant applications and research impact metrics. I can help you set up an open researcher and contributor identification (ORCID), a Biosketch, a PubMed Bibliography, or SciENcv.

Social Media Ambassador

If you have an evidence-based healthcare or STEM conference or event, I will tweet evidence and links from presentations with a conference hashtag for easy discovery.
